# 49 Processes of Traditional Project Management - Data Schema

## 1. Entity Definitions (Dictionary)
*   **Process Groups: A Practice Guide**: PMI publication (2023) containing the 49 processes formerly found in the PMBOK Guide 6th Edition; focuses on predictive life cycles.
*   **Good Practice**: A general consensus that the application of knowledge, skills, tools, and techniques to project management processes can enhance the chance of success over many projects; implies processes are recommended, not mandatory.
*   **ITTO**: Acronym for Inputs, Tools, Techniques, and Outputs; the constituent components of a project management process.
*   **Tailoring**: The act of selecting the appropriate project management processes, inputs, tools, techniques, outputs, and life cycle phases to manage a specific project.
