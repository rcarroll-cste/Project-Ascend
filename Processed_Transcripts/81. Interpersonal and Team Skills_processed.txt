# Interpersonal and Team Skills - Data Schema

## 1. Entity Definitions (Dictionary)
* **Interpersonal and Team Skills**: Soft skills used to manage stakeholders and team members effectively; considered a critical tool used throughout all 49 project management processes.
* **Active Listening**: The act of understanding, acknowledging, and clarifying what others are saying to ensure true comprehension rather than just hearing.
* **Conflict Management**: The process of identifying, de-escalating, and resolving disagreements between team members by finding the root cause of the conflict.
* **Facilitation**: The art of managing a group of people to generate ideas, solve problems, and ensure the team interacts effectively.
* **Meeting Management**: Techniques used to ensure meetings are productive and time-efficient, minimizing waste and maximizing value.

## 2. Process Architecture (ITTOs)
* *Note: Interpersonal and Team Skills is a Tool & Technique used across multiple processes, not a standalone process.*

## 3. Logic & Constraints (Rules Engine)
* **Constraint**: Productive Meetings REQUIRE Defined Agenda.
* **Constraint**: Productive Meetings REQUIRE Invited Stakeholders be limited to those with necessary input or decision-making power.
* **Constraint**: Productive Meetings REQUIRE Enforced Time Limits.
* **Sequence Rule**: Meeting Conclusion -> Followed by distribution of Meeting Minutes AND Action Items.
* **Performance Rule**: Effective Facilitation -> Reduces probability of Team Conflict.

## 5. Semantic Associations (Metadata)
* Interpersonal Skills :: Soft Skills.
* Facilitation :: Managing a group (Generating ideas/Solving problems).
* Active Listening :: Clarifying and Comprehending (not just mind wandering).
* Conflict Management :: Root Cause Analysis for people issues.
* Selective Memory :: Justification for written Action Items/Minutes.
* Meetings without Value :: Waste (Painters not painting / Programmers not coding).
